Flapjack Recipe

Description

These delicious pastries are an old english favourite but ofter referered to in english as crumpets or pancakes. In South Africa we call them Flapjacks and we know to reserve them for special occations or weekends only due to the high cholesterol and sugar content.

Nutrition Per Serving: 158 calories; protein 4.5g; carbohydrates 21.7g; fat 5.9g; cholesterol 37.7mg; sodium 503.6mg. Full Nutrition

Ingredients

  • 1.5cup all-purpose flour
  • 3.5teaspoon baking powder
  • 0.25teaspoon salt
  • 1tablespoon white sugar
  • 1.25cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3tablespoon butter (melted)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
